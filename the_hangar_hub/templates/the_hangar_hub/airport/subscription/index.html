Frequently Asked Questions

What happens during the free trial?
You get full access to all features. After the trial, you can choose to continue with a paid subscription or your airport will return to an inactive status.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
Will I find bugs in the site?
Absolutely. This is a brand-new product, and there will certainly be issues that I had not anticipated. The early adopter plan includes a lifetime low price to thank you for your willingness to help me work through the bugs and make Hangar Hub an indispensable tool for GA airports across the country.
