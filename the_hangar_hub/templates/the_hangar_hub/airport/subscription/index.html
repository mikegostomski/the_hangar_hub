Frequently Asked Questions

What happens during the free trial?
You get full access to all features for 30 days with no credit card required. After the trial, you can choose to continue with a paid subscription or your account will revert to view-only mode.
Can I switch between plans?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you switch from monthly to yearly, we'll prorate your current subscription and apply it to your new plan.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover) as well as ACH bank transfers for annual subscriptions.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
Do you offer discounts for multiple airports?
Yes! If you manage multiple airports, contact our sales team for volume pricing. We offer special rates for airport networks and management companies.
